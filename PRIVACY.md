# Privacy Policy for BetterLectio

**Last updated:** January 10, 2026

## Overview

BetterLectio is a browser extension that enhances the visual appearance and functionality of Lectio (lectio.dk). This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

## Data Collection

**BetterLectio does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.**

### What the extension accesses

- **Lectio pages**: The extension only runs on lectio.dk domains to modify the appearance and add features
- **Local storage**: We use browser local storage to save your preferences (like sidebar state). This data never leaves your browser

### What we do NOT do

- We do not collect analytics or usage data
- We do not track your browsing activity
- We do not access your Lectio credentials
- We do not transmit any data to external servers
- We do not use cookies for tracking

## Permissions Explained

- **storage**: Used to save your local preferences (e.g., sidebar collapsed state, theme settings). All data stays in your browser

## Third Parties

BetterLectio does not share any data with third parties because we do not collect any data.

## Open Source

BetterLectio is open source. You can review the complete source code at:
https://github.com/jonbng/betterlectio

## Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above.
